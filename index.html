About us

Mega Pet is a website where the main function is to advertise pet shelters adoption of pets, in order to reach more people and thus way to contribute to fewer homeless animals. On the other hand we know that hostels often go through money problems so they can't always pay for the maintenance of pets, therefore a function has been added with which the adopters could donate money and implements that would allow the shelters subsist in a better way. We are Megapet!

Within what is expected with this website is the increase in pet adoptions, in turn that hostels have a range of reach greater than they would have without using the web. We wait that the web be recognized as a trusted site where hostels can publish the pets in need. In this way, plan to help eliminate animal abandonment, since According to the information we were able to find, it is too common to see very badly injured animals. or abandoned on the streets. Finally, it should be emphasized that "MegaPet" is an undertaking without for profit which only seek to combat animal abandonment.
